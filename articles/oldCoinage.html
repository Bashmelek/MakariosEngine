Old Coinage



Who this is for:

I began thinking more deeply about coinage in medieval times when I read the fanciful article The Wizards and the Sheep. Fun read, yet I was struck by, for all of its thought and well developed economics of production, it glossed over currency and assumed a DnD system of 10 copper > 1 silver, 10 silver > 1 gold! Probably because it is a DnD blog.
This page is a collection of things I have found in my research on old coinage and money systems, to help gain a better understanding of how these things worked in day to day life in the past. It is for anyone who is interested in history or creating a fantasy world of their own. Focus will be on medieval Europe, but certainly not restricted to that time nor place.


Required Background:

None. You know about coins enough.
